BROADBEACH COASTLINE

Picture-perfect Broadbeach, located 10 minutes south of Surfers Paradise, is centered around Kurrawa Beach, Framed by pristine white sand, lifeguard-patrolled beaches, and adjacent all-ability playground and park, it’s easy to see why Broadbeach foreshore is a favourite hotspot for families. Don’t miss beachfront arts and craft local markets, yoga classes, surf lessons, and an array of water sports to be enjoyed by all ages.

TRANSPORT, G-LINK LIGHT RAIL

G:link, also known as the Gold Coast Light Rail, is a light rail system serving the Gold Coast and is the sole light rail system in Queensland. The system forms part of the TransLink’s South East Queensland public transport network and consists of a single 20 km line of nineteen stations.

EDUCATION

In parallel with the fast population growth of the Gold Coast, there has also been strong expansion in the quality and number of Gold Coast schools, both public and private. The Gold Coast is a fantastic place for tertiary study with Griffith and Bond University both with excellent campuses. There are also many TAFE campuses as well as other higher learning centres.

GOLD COAST HINTERLAND

Just 30 minutes from the beach to reach the Gold Coast hinterland, home to a staggering 100,000 hectares (250,000 acres) of UNESCO World Heritage-listed rainforest. Wild valleys, waterfalls and mountain peaks characterise the region, with forward-thinking culinary offerings (distilleries and wineries included) set among walking trails and wellness resorts.

SURFERS PARADISE CBD

In the lively Surfers Paradise, skyscrapers overlook the sand, creating a dramatic skyline. The beach holiday capital of the coast, this area is well-known for its bars and restaurants where you can make the most of the city’s famous sunshine. Join in with the active locals on a walk through large, waterside parks and along beachfront promenades dotted with street art and trendy cafés.

Lifestyle

Broadbeach is an established Gold Coast icon that combines amazing world-class dining and shopping with the region’s trademark golden sand beaches and glistening sapphire surf.

Its combination of tree-lined streets, connoisseur hotspots and genuine café culture, along with refreshing ocean hues, river breezes and green open spaces, makes Broadbeach one of the most exclusive and sought-after of Gold Coast’s suburbs.

HIGH-END RESTAURANTS & CAFES

Broadbeach is one of the Gold Coast’s central destinations for fine dining from brunch to dinner and for all occasions. Try the local restaurants – Social Eating House on Oracle Avenue, the classy Miss Moneypenny, or venture a short way to the gittering Surfers Paradise or Marina Mirage in Main Beach.

KURRAWA SURF CLUB

The Kurrawa Surf Life Saving Club sits at the heart of the Gold Coast community, where recreational functions are held in a place where the city meets the beach. The building creates a new home for the Surf Life Saving community - patrol members, nippers and for the supporters who can enjoy the 600 seat restaurant, café, bar and gaming lounge along a prime beach frontage.

THE STAR CASINO

The Star Gold Coast (formerly Jupiters Hotel and Casino) is a casino and hotel located in Broadbeach. With its luxurious accommodation and spa, eight iconic bars, seven restaurants, a conference centre and entertainment precincts, alongside the thrills of a world-class casino, The Star Gold Coast is Australia’s five-star fun-parlour.

PACIFIC FAIR SHOPPING CENTRE

Pacific Fair is a major shopping centre in Broadbeach Waters on the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia. An unmissable shopping & dining destination with 400 stores including the best collection of Australian fashion, luxury and global mega brands.

GOLD COAST THEME PARKS

From the world’s tallest and fastest thrill rides, to parks where you can see native Australian wildlife, or splash around in swimming pools and water slides; you’ll find it all on the Gold Coast. Meet your favourite movie characters and super-heroes; or swim with dolphins and sharks.
